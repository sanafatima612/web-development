About Punjab University Gujranwala Campus

It is for the first time in 124-year history of Punjab University that it has established a teaching campus outside Lahore.

The Gujranwala Campus has been established with the keen interest and support of the Honourable and Worthy Vice-Chancellor. The opening of the sub-campus is in accordance with the educational policy of the President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, who is committed to promoting education.

Gujranwala Campus is located near Alipur Chowk, Rawalpindi By-pass, and covers an area of 81 Kanals. It features 37 classrooms, 1 administration block, a fully air-conditioned library, a multi-purpose hall, and facilities like car parking, a canteen, a bookshop, and grassy lawns.
